Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for admission?

You can fill out the admission form online through our portal or visit the college office for assistance.

How do I submit my exam form?

Exam forms can be submitted online through the university website, or you can visit our helpdesk for guidance.

Where can I download my hall ticket?

Hall tickets are available on the official university website. We also provide step-by-step instructions for downloading.

How can I check my exam results?

Results are published online on the university portal. You can enter your roll number to access your scorecard.
